Teriyaki Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon Filet (2 lbs)
  • Soy Sauce (1/2 cup)
  • Rice Vinegar (2 tbsp)
  • Water (2 tbsp)
  • Sesame Oil (2 tsp)
  • Brown Sugar (3 tbsp)
  • Fresh Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Fresh Ginger (1 tbsp)
  • Arrowroot Powder or Cornstarch (1tsp)
  • Sesame Seeds

Recipe

Mix together soy sauce, rice vinegar, water, sesame oil, brown sugar, garlic and ginger.

Add salmon filets to a bowl with half of the marinade. We will use the rest of the marinade at the end to glaze the salmon.

Next, preheat pan on a stove at medium heat and add salmon skin-side down when ready. Use oil to ensure the skin doesn't stick.

Let the salmon cook for 6-8 minutes, and using a spatula flip the salmon over. Once flipped, cook for an addition 2-3 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 55 degree celcius. Once cooked, let rest for an additonal 10 minutes.

Heat the remaining marinade in a sauce pan over medium heat, once bubbling, mix arrowroot powder with 1 tbsp water and let it cook until the marinade has thickened. Turn off heat and brush onto salmon as desired.

Finally, top fish with sesame seeds and enjoy.
