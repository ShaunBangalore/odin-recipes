Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats (1/2 cup)
  • Milk (1/2 cup)
  • Chia Seeds (1/8 cup)
  • Greek Yogurt (1/2 cup)
  • Vanilla Extract (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Toppings (Optional)

Recipe

Add oats to your preferred container or bowl and combine with chia seeds, yogurt, vanilla extract, salt and any other optional toppings.

Then top with milk and stir well until no clumps remain. Next, seal or cover your container with a lid and store in fridge overnight or for a minimum of 2 hours.

Enjoy once chilled.
