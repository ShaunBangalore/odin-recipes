Grilled Chicken Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Shredded Chicken (2 cups)
  • Cream Cheese (1 block)
  • Buffalo Sauce (1 cup)
  • Shredded White Cheddar (1/2 cup)
  • Butter (2 tbsp)
  • 4 Slices of Bread

Recipe

In a large bowl, combine chicken, cream cheese, buffalo sauce, and white cheddar cheese. Slather on bread and form into sandwiches, set aside.

On a saute pan over medium heat, add butter and let melt. Add sandwiches and let cook until golden brown on both sides. (1-2 minutes each)

Once cooked, let it cool and then slice into prefered shape and size. Enjoy.
